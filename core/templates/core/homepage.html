Rules
  • Access & Security

    Only use your official college credentials to access the portal. Keep your login information private and secure. Sharing accounts or logging in on behalf of someone else is strictly prohibited.

  • Fair Exam Conduct

    All exams must be taken honestly and independently. Using unauthorized resources (notes, other websites, devices, or help from others) is not allowed. If required, keep your camera and microphone on for monitoring purposes.

  • Respectful Communication

    Always communicate respectfully with faculty, staff, and fellow students. Inappropriate language, harassment, or disruptive behavior in any form will not be tolerated on the platform.

  • Timely Submissions

    Submit all assignments, quizzes, and exams within the given deadlines. Late submissions may result in penalties unless prior approval has been granted for valid reasons.

  • Use of Authorized Devices

    Only use devices that have been approved or are compliant with college guidelines. Avoid using multiple devices during assessments unless explicitly allowed.

  • Academic Integrity

    Plagiarism, impersonation, or any form of cheating is a serious offense. Ensure all submitted work is your own and cite sources where applicable.

  • Technical Preparedness

    Ensure your device is fully charged and your internet connection is stable before beginning any online session or assessment. Keep backup options ready if possible.
